The project in one sentence

A fully automated pipeline producing publication-ready Relevo.com sports articles (research → drafting → editorial review → image → SEO → publish), shipping ~50 articles/day at $0.10–0.30 per article, ready for the FIFA World Cup 2026 traffic window.

Where we are today

  • Technical substrate complete (Phases 1–3). Pipeline is end-to-end functional: SharePoint inventory, image generation via LiteLLM, WordPress publishing, editorial hand-off through Asana, public roadmap, full audit trail in the official repository.
  • Editorial substrate at style-guide v3. Two reviewer rounds with Joel Sierra absorbed. The bolds-coverage rule (≥80% of paragraphs carry a highlighted idea) was tuned today after live validation on the beta WordPress render.
  • 11 articles regenerated under the latest style. 2 already signed off by the reviewer; the remaining 9 went out as WordPress drafts tonight under the v1.1.3 manual and the new editorial gates.
  • Critical timeline. Hard public launch: June 1. FIFA World Cup kickoff: June 11.
