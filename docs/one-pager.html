Content strategy — four verticals

Relevo.com was a sports-journalism outlet (2022-2025) acquired by iGaming.com to be repositioned as a media surface that advertises offers for bettors. The automation MVP is the production layer of that repositioning. Business hypothesis (JPW, 26/5): evergreen alone won't sustain Relevo — the audience does not return to a static-content media; current-affairs content tied to a sports calendar is the traffic engine.

The Inventory now organises every article along one cardinal axis — article_type — with four fixed values:

  • evergreen — the timeless base. Four sub-categories: rules, tactics, origins, coaches. Style guide v1.2.1 applies. Status: 175 rows + 20 coaches seeded (26/5); pipeline live; first coaches smoke (Simeone) in Joel's queue.
  • news — anticipatory, calendar-driven. NOT post-event reporting (no editorial team for that). Pre-event articles tied to the sports calendar H2 2026 (~50 marquee events compiled 26/5). Status: 0 rows; awaiting TEMPLATE-05 design after Joel's calendar review.
  • casino — brand reviews, payment methods, ranking pages (reference: estafa.info, casinos-online.es). Requires compliance review + disclaimers + geo-gating. Status: 0 rows; TEMPLATE-06 pending stakeholder alignment.
  • sportsbetting — picks per match with fundamentals/rationale. Blocked on Giannina's calls: data API to contract, publishing cadence, article format. Status: 0 rows; TEMPLATE-07 pending Giannina decisions.

Each vertical has its own style guide and review pipeline. v1.2.1 is not generalised to news/casino/sportsbetting — those will land separate. Compliance separation, lifecycle separation, reviewer routing and revenue tracking all justify the axis. See calendar for the news anchor.
